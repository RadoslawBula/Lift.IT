1. What is LIFT IT?
LIFT IT is a fitness tracking platform designed to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. You can log your workouts, caffeine intake, and water consumption while earning achievements and receiving personalized feedback on your training sessions.

3. How do I log my workouts?
Navigate to the "Workout Log" section. Select the type of exercise, input the relevant details (such as sets, reps, and weights), and save your session. You can also review past workouts in your activity history.
4. Can I track my water and caffeine intake?
Yes! Go to the "Caffeine log" and ‚ÄúWater tracker‚Äù sections to log your daily water and caffeine intake. The app will provide reminders and suggest optimal intake levels based on your goals.
5. What are achievements?
Achievements are rewards for reaching specific fitness milestones. You can earn them by completing challenges, maintaining consistent workout streaks, and achieving personal bests in various exercises.
6. How does personalized feedback work
LIFT IT analyzes your workout data and progress over time. Based on your performance, it offers customized tips, workout suggestions, and training adjustments to help you reach your goals faster.
7. Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and use industry-standard encryption to secure your data. You can review our full privacy policy for more details.
8. How do I reset my password?
If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password securely.
9. How do I contact customer support?
You can contact us through the "Support" section in the app or send an email to support@liftit.com. Our team is ready to assist you with any questions or issues.
